Simpson's Family

The Simpsons is an American adult animated sitcom created by Matt Groening for the Fox Broadcasting Company.[1][2][3] The series is a satirical depiction of American life, epitomized by the Simpson family, which consists of Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie.

Homer

Homer Jay Simpson is one of the main characters of the American animated sitcom The Simpsons.

Marge

Marge is the matriarch of the Simpson family. With her husband Homer, she has three children: Bart, Lisa, and Maggie.

Bart

Bartholomew Jo-Jo "Bart" Simpson is a character from the animated series The Simpsons . Along with Homer , one of the show's more well-known characters. In the list of the fifty best cartoon characters in history, according to TV Guide magazine, he ranks 11th along with his sister Lisa

Lisa

Lisa Marie Simpson[1] is a fictional character in the animated television series The Simpsons. She is the middle child and most accomplished of the Simpson family
